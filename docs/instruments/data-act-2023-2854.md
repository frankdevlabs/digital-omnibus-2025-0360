Amended by **Article 1** of the Omnibus — but **only in the April text, ST 8261/1/26 REV 1**. February
(ST 6406/26) and May (ST 9547/26) contain no Omnibus Article 1; the Data Act is merely cover-listed there.
Operative text (April):
[../../extracts/council/ST-8261-1-26-REV1_dataact-art1.md](../../extracts/council/ST-8261-1-26-REV1_dataact-art1.md).

## What Article 1 does

The Omnibus turns the Data Act into the **consolidating home** for several data-acquis instruments that
Article 10 repeals (the **Data Governance Act (EU) 2022/868**, the **Free Flow of Non-Personal Data
Regulation (EU) 2018/1807** and the **Open Data Directive (EU) 2019/1024** — see
[repealed instruments](repealed.md)). It amends a set of existing Data Act articles (Article 1 scope,
Article 2 definitions, Articles 17–21, Article 32) and **inserts three new chapters**:

| Inserted block | Articles | Subject (high level) |
|---|---|---|
| **Chapter VIIa** | 32a–32g | Data intermediation services (relocated from the DGA) |
| **Chapter VIIb** | 32h | Data altruism (relocated from the DGA) |
| **Chapter VIIc** | 32i–32z | Re-use of certain categories of protected data held by public sector bodies / open data (relocated from the DGA and the Open Data Directive), the European Data Innovation Board, and related procedures |

## Advisory positions

- **ECB (CON/2026/9)** — welcomes simplification of G2B/B2G data sharing (NCBs and the ECB benefit from
  clearer access to **high-value datasets** used for statistics); supports **narrowing** the new
  "exceptional need" power (Chapter VIIc / Art 15a) but asks to **simplify the conditions** public
  authorities must meet and to **clarify "public emergency"** (align with Reg (EU) 2024/2747 IMERA); on
  compensation, public-interest data should be supplied **at cost**, without a "reasonable margin".
  → [digest](../advisory/ecb-con-2026-9.md)
- **EESC (INT/1108)** — mandatory data-sharing should be limited to where **strictly necessary** (public
  emergencies, workers'/fundamental-rights protection); the Commission should prioritise **incentives for
  voluntary sharing**, **strengthen the European Data Innovation Board**, and foster common European data
  spaces. → [digest](../advisory/eesc-int1108.md)
