# Instrument: NIS2 Directive — Directive (EU) 2022/2555 (+ DORA, CER, eIDAS)

Amended by **Articles 6–9** of the Omnibus. Operative text:
[../../extracts/council/ST-9547-2026_cyber-art6-9.md](../../extracts/council/ST-9547-2026_cyber-art6-9.md).

## NIS2 (Art 6 of the Omnibus)
- **New Art 23a** — ENISA *incident-reporting information point* (identify obligations + redirect; does
  not store notifications).
- **New Art 23b** — Member State *national entry points* (receive reports; interoperable).
- **New Art 23c** — harmonising incident-notification framework (Commission report + ENISA guidelines).
- **New Art 23d** — cross-border incident notification.
- **Art 23 / Art 30** — notify significant incidents via the national entry point.

## Consequential routing amendments
- **eIDAS — Reg (EU) 910/2014** (Art 7): Arts 19a(1a), 24(2a), 45a(3a) — notify via national entry point.
- **DORA — Reg (EU) 2022/2554** (Art 8): Art 19(1)/(2) — major ICT incidents & cyber threats via the
  national entry point. *Relevant for financial entities.*
- **CER — Dir (EU) 2022/2557** (Art 9): Art 15(1) — notify via national entry point; new implementing-act
  power on format.

See [single-entry-point provision](../provisions/cyber-single-entry-point.md). Switch-over: **30 months**
from entry into force ([final provisions extract](../../extracts/council/ST-9547-2026_final-art10-11.md);
