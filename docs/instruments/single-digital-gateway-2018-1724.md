# Instrument: Single Digital Gateway — Regulation (EU) 2018/1724

Amended by **Article 2** of the Omnibus — but **only in the April text, ST 8261/1/26 REV 1**. February
(ST 6406/26) and May (ST 9547/26) contain no Omnibus Article 2. Operative text (April):
[../../extracts/council/ST-8261-1-26-REV1_sdg-art2.md](../../extracts/council/ST-8261-1-26-REV1_sdg-art2.md).

## What Article 2 does

A single amendment: in the table in **Annex II** to Regulation (EU) 2018/1724, the **"Starting, running
and closing a business"** life-event entry is **replaced**. The replaced entry's list of procedures now
includes **registration as a data intermediation services provider** and **as a data altruism organisation
recognised in the Union** — registrations that originate in the Data Governance Act (repealed by Omnibus
Article 10) and whose substance moves into the Data Act (Omnibus Article 1, Chapters VIIa–VIIb). See the
[Data Act instrument page](data-act-2023-2854.md) and [repealed instruments](repealed.md).
