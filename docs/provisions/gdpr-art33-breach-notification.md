# GDPR Art 33 — Personal-data-breach notification

**Tracked because:** changes both the **threshold** and the **deadline** and routes notification through
the new single-entry point. Directly operational for regulated entities.

## Current law
Art 33(1): notify the DPA without undue delay and where feasible within **72 hours**, unless the breach is
unlikely to result in a risk. Art 33(5): document all breaches.

## Commission proposal
Threshold raised to **high risk** only; deadline extended to **96 hours**; notify via the single-entry
point; EDPB common template + high-risk lists.

## Council — ST 9547/26
- **Retained.** High-risk-only threshold; **96 hours**; via the **national entry point** (Art 23b NIS2);
  interim direct-notification until the entry point exists (new Art 33(1a)).
- **EDPB** establishes/publishes the common template **and** lists of high-risk / not-high-risk
  circumstances (new Art 33(6)–(7)); Commission may adopt the template by implementing act.
