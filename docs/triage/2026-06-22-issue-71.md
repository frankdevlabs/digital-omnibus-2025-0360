## Assessment

**Out of scope for 2025/0360 → recommend closing the issue as not-relevant; no repo edits.**

- **Wrong file.** The interinstitutional subject of both documents is the **DSA minors-online fiche
  (Kamerstuk 22112-4207)**, not the data/cyber Digital Omnibus 2025/0360 (nor even the AI-Act file
  2025/0359). The *Digitale Omnibus(sen)* appears only as cross-cutting overlap-context, not as the
  document's topic. The Dutch-language scope rule (`T1-16`) admits an item *only if it concerns the
  Omnibus Digitaal* — this one concerns a different file.
- **No new substance.** The omnibus passage merely restates the **already-recorded** general NL line
  (the Omnibus must not undermine the objectives of the legislation it amends; streamline reporting
  duties/definitions; strengthen governance) — exactly the line captured from the 8 April non-paper and
  the 18 June Kamerbrief in [`member-state-positions.md`](../member-state-positions.md). It tables no new
  position and reports no new procedural fact on 2025/0360.
- **No new primary document.** The non-papers it cites are pre-existing: the Oct/Nov 2025 non-papers
  attached to the verslag of the informal Telecom Council of 9-10 Oct 2025 (sent to the Kamer 12 Nov
  2025) and the cabinet's BNC-fiche; the NL non-paper / Kamerbrief stream is already committed
  (`NL-NONPAPER-2026-04-08`, `NL-KAMERBRIEF-2026-06-18`). Nothing here is a new committable
  member-state position document for this file.
- **Distinct from the recorded precedents.** Unlike #38 (TK *debate reports on the Omnibus*) and #51
  (Commission *reaction letter on the Omnibus*) — procedural reportage that was *about* the file and so
  cited in TIMELINE/positions — these documents are *about another file* and only mention the Omnibus in
  passing. Recording them would put an off-file (DSA-minors) item into the 2025/0360 chronology.
