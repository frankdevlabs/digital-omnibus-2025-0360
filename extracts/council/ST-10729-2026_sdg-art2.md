**What this is.** Omnibus **Article 2** makes a single amendment to the **Single Digital Gateway
Regulation (EU) 2018/1724** (p. 110 of the PDF): it replaces the **Annex II** "Starting, running and
closing a business" life-event entry. The amendment first appeared in the April text (ST 8261/1/26 REV 1)
and is carried — substantively unchanged — into this mandate text; it is **absent** from the February
(ST 6406/26) and May (ST 9547/26) compromise texts, which do not amend the SDG. See
[`single-digital-gateway-2018-1724.md`](../../docs/instruments/single-digital-gateway-2018-1724.md).

## Article 2 — Annex II "Starting, running and closing a business" entry replaced

*"In the table in Annex II to Regulation (EU) 2018/1724, the entry 'Starting, running and closing a
business' is replaced by the following:"* (consolidated):

| Life events | Procedures | Expected output |
|---|---|---|
| Starting, running and closing a business | Notification of business activity, permission for exercising a business activity, changes of business activity and the termination of a business activity not involving insolvency or liquidation procedures, excluding the initial registration of a business activity with the business register and excluding procedures concerning the constitution of or any subsequent filing by companies or firms within the meaning of the second paragraph of Article 54 TFEU | Confirmation of the receipt of notification or change, or of the request for permission for business activity subject to an assessment of the application by the competent authority in accordance with national law, where relevant |
| | Registration of an employer (a natural person) with compulsory pension and insurance schemes | Confirmation of registration or social security registration number |
| | Registration of employees with compulsory pension and insurance schemes | Confirmation of registration or social security registration number |
| | Submitting a corporate tax declaration | Confirmation of the receipt of the declaration |
| | Notification to the social security schemes of the end of contract with an employee, excluding procedures for the collective termination of employee contracts | Confirmation of the receipt of the notification |
| | Payment of social contributions for employees | Receipt or other form of confirmation of payment of social contributions for employees |
| | Registration as a data intermediation services provider | Confirmation of the registration |
| | Registration as a data altruism organisation recognised in the Union | Confirmation of the registration |

▸ **Unchanged in substance vs ST 8261/1/26 REV 1** (April). The entry is **replaced wholesale**; the
substantively notable additions are the two final procedure rows — **registration as a data
intermediation services provider** and **as a data altruism organisation recognised in the Union**.
These registration regimes originate in the **Data Governance Act (Reg (EU) 2022/868)**, which Omnibus
Article 10 **repeals**; their substance is relocated into the **Data Act** (Reg (EU) 2023/2854) via
Omnibus Article 1 ([Data Act extract](ST-10729-2026_dataact-art1.md) — see
[Chapter VIIa](ST-10729-2026_dataact-art1.md#chapter-viia) and
[Article 32e](ST-10729-2026_dataact-art1.md#article-32e)), and the Single Digital Gateway's business
life-event procedure list is updated here to surface those registrations. See
[repealed-instruments page](../../docs/instruments/repealed.md).

▸ **vs ST 9547/26 (May).** May contained **no Article 2** at all (the SDG was not amended) — see
[ST 9547/26 SDG (absent)](ST-9547-2026_sdg-art2.md). This mandate text reinstates the April amendment.
