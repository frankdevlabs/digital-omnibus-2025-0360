# Extract — ST 6406/26, Article 1 (Data Act, Reg. (EU) 2023/2854) — no operative amendment in this text

> **Source:** Council document **ST 6406/26** (LIMITE), Brussels, 20 February 2026 — Presidency
> compromise text, for the AGS meeting (Antici Group (Simplification)) of 27 February 2026.
> Interinstitutional file 2025/0360 (COD). **This is a working transcription, not an official text** —
> consolidated reading. Always verify against the authoritative document. See [`../../NOTICE`](../../NOTICE).

**This section is absent as operative text in the February compromise.** ST 6406/26 contains **no Omnibus
Article 1** and therefore **no amendment to the Data Act, Regulation (EU) 2023/2854**. The document's
subject line is "Omnibus VII – Digital (GDPR/P2B)" and its enacting terms run from **Article 3** (GDPR) to
**Article 10** (repeals) — there is no Article 1 (Data Act) or Article 2 (Single Digital Gateway). The Data
Act is only **cover-listed** among the acts the Omnibus eventually amends.

▸ **Feb → Apr movement.** The Data Act amendments — Omnibus **Article 1**, inserting Chapters VIIa–VIIc /
Articles 32a–32z into Regulation (EU) 2023/2854 — first appear in the **April** text (ST 8261/1/26 REV 1).
See [`ST-8261-1-26-REV1_dataact-art1.md`](ST-8261-1-26-REV1_dataact-art1.md) for the substance, and the
[Data Act instrument page](../../docs/instruments/data-act-2023-2854.md). The repeal of the Data Governance
Act / Free Flow of Non-Personal Data Regulation / Open Data Directive (whose substance the April Data Act
chapters absorb) is likewise **absent** in February — see
[final provisions](ST-6406-2026_final-art10-11.md) and the
[repealed-instruments page](../../docs/instruments/repealed.md).
