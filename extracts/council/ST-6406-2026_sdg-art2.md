# Extract — ST 6406/26, Article 2 (Single Digital Gateway, Reg. (EU) 2018/1724) — no operative amendment in this text

> **Source:** Council document **ST 6406/26** (LIMITE), Brussels, 20 February 2026 — Presidency
> compromise text, for the AGS meeting (Antici Group (Simplification)) of 27 February 2026.
> Interinstitutional file 2025/0360 (COD). **This is a working transcription, not an official text** —
> consolidated reading. Always verify against the authoritative document. See [`../../NOTICE`](../../NOTICE).

**This section is absent as operative text in the February compromise.** ST 6406/26 contains **no Omnibus
Article 2** and therefore **no amendment to the Single Digital Gateway Regulation (EU) 2018/1724**. The
February enacting terms run from **Article 3** (GDPR) to **Article 10** (repeals).

▸ **Feb → Apr movement.** The SDG amendment — Omnibus **Article 2**, replacing the Annex II "Starting,
running and closing a business" life-event entry (to surface data-intermediation and data-altruism
registration procedures) — first appears in the **April** text (ST 8261/1/26 REV 1). See
[`ST-8261-1-26-REV1_sdg-art2.md`](ST-8261-1-26-REV1_sdg-art2.md) and the
[SDG instrument page](../../docs/instruments/single-digital-gateway-2018-1724.md).
